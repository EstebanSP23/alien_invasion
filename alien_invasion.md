# Alien Invasion

A simple **Space Invaders–style game** built with Python and Pygame.  
Shoot down aliens and survive as long as possible!  

---

## How to Play
- Use the **arrow keys** to move your ship left and right.
- Press **Spacebar** to fire bullets.
- Aliens move faster as the game progresses.
- The game ends if the aliens reach your ship.

---

## Requirements
- Python 3.10+  
- [Pygame](https://www.pygame.org/)
